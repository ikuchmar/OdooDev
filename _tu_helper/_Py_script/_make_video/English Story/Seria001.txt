Seria001 — United Kingdom

1.1 The United Kingdom.

EN:
United Kingdom: The United Kingdom.
The United Kingdom is a country made of four parts.
It includes England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland.
Its full name is “The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland.”
The capital is London, the center of politics and business.
The UK has a population of about 67 million.
It is a constitutional monarchy with a king or queen.
Parliament makes the laws in the UK.
The UK has a long history of empire and trade.
It is still important in world politics today.
The UK is also known for music, films, and sports.
Many tourists visit cities, castles, and countryside across the UK.
The United Kingdom is not the same as Great Britain.
Knowing the difference helps people understand the country.

EN:

It does not include Northern Ireland.
The term “Great Britain” describes only the island.
It is the largest island in Europe.
Great Britain has mountains, rivers, and many cities.
London is the biggest city, but Edinburgh and Cardiff are also important.
The name is often confused with the United Kingdom.
Great Britain has about 67 million people.
It played a key role in history and world politics.
The island is a center of culture, language, and trade.
Many famous inventions came from Great Britain.
It remains one of the most influential places in the world.
Understanding the name helps people use it correctly.

1.3 England.

EN:
United Kingdom: England.
England is a country in the south of Great Britain.
It shares borders with Scotland to the north and Wales to the west.
The capital of England is London, one of the largest cities in the world.
England has many rivers, hills, and green fields.
It is famous for its old castles, universities, and football clubs.
The population is about 56 million people.
English is the main language spoken there.
England is known for writers like Shakespeare and scientists like Newton.
It is also the birthplace of the Industrial Revolution.
Many traditions, such as afternoon tea, come from England.
Tourists visit places like Stonehenge, Oxford, and Windsor Castle.
England is the largest country in the United Kingdom.
It plays a central role in politics, culture, and history.

UA:
Об’єднане Королівство: Утворення Об’єднаного Королівства.
Об’єднане Королівство не було створене в один момент.
Його частини об’єднувалися поступово.
У 1536 році Уельс був об’єднаний з Англією за законом.
У 1603 році корони Англії та Шотландії з’єдналися, коли король Яків VI Шотландський став Яковом I Англії.
У 1707 році Акт про унію створив Велику Британію, об’єднавши Англію та Шотландію.
У 1801 році була приєднана Ірландія, і країна отримала назву Об’єднане Королівство Великої Британії та Ірландії.
Пізніше, у 1921 році, більша частина Ірландії стала незалежною.
Північна Ірландія залишилася у складі Об’єднаного Королівства.
Унії не завжди були повністю добровільними.
Деякі частини об’єднувалися за згодою, інші — під тиском чи політикою.
Сьогодні UK показує довгу історію союзів і змін.
