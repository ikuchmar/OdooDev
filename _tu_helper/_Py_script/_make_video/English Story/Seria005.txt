5.1 Roman Britain.

EN:
Roman Britain.
Before the Romans came, Britain was home to Celtic tribes.
They lived in villages and farmed the land.
They built hillforts for protection.
Druids were important priests in their culture.
The Romans invaded Britain in 43 AD.
They built roads, towns, and Hadrian’s Wall.
Baths and villas showed Roman culture.
Latin mixed with local languages.
The Romans stayed for almost 400 years.
Their influence is still visible today.
